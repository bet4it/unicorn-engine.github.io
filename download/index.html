Community bindings

Besides Haskell, Ruby, Python, Java, Go, .NET & MSVC supported in the main code, some bindings for other languages are created and maintained by the community.

  • UnicornPascal: Delphi/Free Pascal binding (by Stievie).
  • Unicorn-Rs: Rust binding (by Sébastien Duquette)
  • UnicornEngine: Perl binding (by Vikas Naresh Kumar)
